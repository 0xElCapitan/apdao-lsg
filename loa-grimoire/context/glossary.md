# apDAO LSG Glossary

**Seat**: Soulbound ERC-721 NFT. One vote per epoch.

**LSG**: Liquid Signal Governance. Weekly voting on revenue allocation.

**Epoch**: Weekly voting period. Votes reset at boundary.

**Revenue Sleeve**: Destination for protocol revenue. Targets strategies.

**Subvalidator**: Vase Finance validator position. Revenue source.

**Bribe**: Incentive mechanism. Synthetix-style accumulator.

**Strategy**: Contract implementing revenue destination logic.

**Accumulator**: Fixed-point math pattern. Rewards accrue monotonically.

**Vase Finance**: Berachain validator/AMM ecosystem partner.

**Owner-Gated**: Only owner (apDAO governance) can invoke.
